Conclusion

So, what now?

Naturally, there is a big difference between reading about living in a vehicle, and actually living in a vehicle. For many “van-dwellers”, vehicle living is not the result of an unfortunate crisis or circumstance, but an active choice to avoid conventional forms of housing, save money, and travel more often. The one common sentiment we’ve heard from our research is that living in a vehicle isn’t nearly as intimidating or dangerous as rumors sometimes imply. With time and experience, you can find yourself in a natural routine, with a comfortable home on wheels, and with a freedom that traditional housing doesn’t afford.

If you still need more information on vehicle living, we encourage you to give our Community Resources page a look. On that page, you can find numerous online resources, sorted by topic and content. Between the Community Resources page, and our search functionality, hopefully you can find the information you need.

In addition, we welcome any help that our users have to offer. Through our feedback form, we can respond to your suggestions and questions, and using our help, improve this website. If you have discovered a useful resource online, and want to share it with other users of this website, don’t hesitate to visit our Community Resources page and click the “Suggest a Resource” button in the top right.

Thanks for reading!
